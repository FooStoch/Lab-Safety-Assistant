Personal Protection/Sanitation: (see Table 2): Skin: Prevent skin contact Eyes: Prevent eye contact Wash skin: When contam Remove: When wet (flamm) Change: N.R. Provide: Eyewash (>15%) Quick drench (>25%). Physical Description: Colorless liquid with a weak, ammonia- or fish-like odor. [Note: A solid below 23°F.].
