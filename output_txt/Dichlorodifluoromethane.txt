Dichlorodifluoromethane – Formula: CCl2F2 (CAS 75-71-8) - Synonyms/Trade Names: Difluorodichloromethane, Fluorocarbon 12, Freon® 12, Genetron® 12, Halon® 122, Propellant 12, Refrigerant 12. IDLH 15,000 ppm | NIOSH REL: TWA 1000 ppm (4950 mg/m 3 ) OSHA PEL: TWA 1000 ppm (4950 mg/m3 ) | Incompatibilities and Reactivities: Chemically-active metals such as sodium, potassium, calcium, powdered aluminum, zinc & magnesium Exposure Routes, Symptoms, Target Organs (see Table 5): ER: Inh, Con (liquid) SY: Dizz, tremor, asphy, uncon, card arrhy, card arrest; liquid: frostbite TO: CVS, PNS First Aid (see Table 6): Eye: Frostbite Skin: Frostbite Breath: Resp support D 1,3-Dichloro-5,5-dimethylhydantoin. Personal Protection/Sanitation: (see Table 2): Skin: Frostbite Eyes: Frostbite Wash skin: N.R. Remove: N.R. Change: N.R. Provide: Frostbite wash. Physical Description: Colorless gas with an ether-like odor at extremely high concentrations. [Note: Shipped as a liquefied compressed gas.].
