Dimethylphthalate – Formula: C6H4(COOCH3)2 (CAS 131-11-3) - Synonyms/Trade Names: Dimethyl ester of 1,2-benzenedicarboxylic acid; DMP. IDLH 2000 mg/m3 | NIOSH REL: TWA 5 mg/m3 OSHA PEL: TWA 5 mg/m3 | Incompatibilities and Reactivities: Nitrates; strong oxidizers, alkalis & acids Exposure Routes, Symptoms, Target Organs (see Table 5): ER: Inh, Ing, Con SY: Irrit eyes, upper resp sys; stomach pain TO: Eyes, resp sys, GI tract First Aid (see Table 6): Eye: Irr prompt Skin: Wash regularly Breath: Resp support Swallow: Medical attention immed D Dimethyl sulfate. Personal Protection/Sanitation: (see Table 2): Skin: N.R. Eyes: Prevent eye contact Wash skin: N.R. Remove: N.R. Change: N.R.. Physical Description: Colorless, oily liquid with a slight, aromatic odor. [Note: A solid below 42°F.].
