Personal Protection/Sanitation: (see Table 2): Skin: Prevent skin contact Eyes: Prevent eye contact Wash skin: When contam Remove: When wet or contam Change: Daily. Physical Description: Colorless to brown solid with an odor of mothballs. [Note: Shipped as a molten solid.].
