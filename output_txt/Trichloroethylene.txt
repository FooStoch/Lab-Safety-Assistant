Trichloroethylene – Formula: ClCH=CCl2 (CAS 79-01-6) - Synonyms/Trade Names: Ethylene trichloride, TCE, Trichloroethene, Trilene. Personal Protection/Sanitation: (see Table 2): Skin: Prevent skin contact Eyes: Prevent eye contact Wash skin: When contam Remove: When wet or contam Change: N.R. Provide: Eyewash Quick drench.
