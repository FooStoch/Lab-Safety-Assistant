Diquat (Diquat dibromide) – Formula: C12H12N2Br2 (CAS 85-00-7) - Synonyms/Trade Names: Diquat dibromide; 1,1'-Ethylene-2,2'-bipyridyllium dibromide [Note: Diquat is a cation (C12H12N2 ++; 1,1'-Ethylene-2,2-bipyridyllium ion). Various diquat salts are commercially available.]. Personal Protection/Sanitation: (see Table 2): Skin: Prevent skin contact Eyes: Prevent eye contact Wash skin: When contam Remove: When wet or contam Change: Daily Provide: Quick drench.
