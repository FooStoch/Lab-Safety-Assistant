Ethylenediamine – Formula: NH2CH2CH2NH2 (CAS 107-15-3) - Synonyms/Trade Names: 1,2-Diaminoethane; 1,2-Ethanediamine; Ethylenediamine (anhydrous). Personal Protection/Sanitation: (see Table 2): Skin: Prevent skin contact Eyes: Prevent eye contact Wash skin: When contam/Daily Remove: When wet (flamm) Change: N.R. Provide: Eyewash (>5%) Quick drench. Physical Description: Colorless, viscous liquid with an ammonia-like odor. [fungicide] [Note: A solid below 47°F.].
