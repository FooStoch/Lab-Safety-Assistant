Personal Protection/Sanitation: (see Table 2): Skin: Prevent skin contact Eyes: Prevent eye contact Wash skin: When contam/Daily Remove: When wet or contam Change: Daily Provide: Eyewash Quick drench. Physical Description: Colorless to pale-yellow solid or liquid (above 71°F) with a sharp, pungent odor..
