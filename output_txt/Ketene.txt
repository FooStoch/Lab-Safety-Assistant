Ketene – Formula: CH2=CO (CAS 463-51-4) - Synonyms/Trade Names: Carbomethene, Ethenone, Keto-ethylene. IDLH 5 ppm | NIOSH REL: TWA 0.5 ppm (0.9 mg/m3 ) ST 1.5 ppm (3 mg/m3 ) OSHA PEL†: TWA 0.5 ppm (0.9 mg/m3 ) | Incompatibilities and Reactivities: Water, alcohols, ammonia [Note: Readily polymerizes. Personal Protection/Sanitation: (see Table 2): Skin: N.R. Eyes: N.R. Wash skin: N.R. Remove: N.R. Change: N.R.. Physical Description: Colorless gas with a penetrating odor..
