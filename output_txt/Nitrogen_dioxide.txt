Nitrogen dioxide – Formula: NO2 (CAS 10102-44-0) - Synonyms/Trade Names: Dinitrogen tetroxide (N2O4), Nitrogen peroxide. Personal Protection/Sanitation: (see Table 2): Skin: Prevent skin contact Eyes: Prevent eye contact Wash skin: When contam Remove: When wet or contam Change: N.R. Provide: Eyewash Quick drench. Physical Description: Yellowish-brown liquid or reddish-brown gas (above 70°F) with a pungent, acrid odor. [Note: In solid form (below 15°F) it is found structurally as N2O4.].
