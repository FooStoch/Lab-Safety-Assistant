Personal Protection/Sanitation: (see Table 2): Skin: Frostbite Eyes: Frostbite Wash skin: N.R. Remove: N.R. Change: N.R. Provide: Frostbite wash. Physical Description: Colorless gas with a characteristic, sweet odor. [Note: Shipped as a liquefied compressed gas.].
