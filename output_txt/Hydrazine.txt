IDLH Ca [50 ppm] | NIOSH REL: Ca C 0.03 ppm (0.04 mg/m3 ) [2-hour] See Appendix A OSHA PEL†: TWA 1 ppm (1.3 mg/m3 ) [skin] | Incompatibilities and Reactivities: Oxidizers, hydrogen peroxide, nitric acid, metallic oxides, acids [Note: Can ignite SPONTANEOUSLY on contact with oxidizers or porous materials such as earth, wood & cloth.] Exposure Routes, Symptoms, Target Organs (see Table 5): ER: Inh, Abs, Ing, Con SY: Irrit eyes, skin, nose, throat; temporary blindness; dizz, nau; derm; eye, skin burns; in animals: bron, pulm edema; liver, kidney damage; convuls; [carc] TO: Eyes, skin, resp sys, CNS, liver, kidneys [in animals: tumors of the lungs, liver, blood vessels & intestine] First Aid (see Table 6): Eye: Irr immed Skin: Water flush immed Breath: Resp support Swallow: Medical attention immed Hydrogenated terphenyls. Personal Protection/Sanitation: (see Table 2): Skin: Prevent skin contact Eyes: Prevent eye contact Wash skin: When contam Remove: When wet (flamm) Change: N.R. Provide: Eyewash Quick drench. Physical Description: Colorless, fuming, oily liquid with an ammonia-like odor. [Note: A solid below 36°F.].
