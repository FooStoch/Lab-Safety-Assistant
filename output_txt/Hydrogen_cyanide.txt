Hydrogen cyanide – Formula: HCN (CAS 74-90-8) - Synonyms/Trade Names: Formonitrile, Hydrocyanic acid, Prussic acid. Personal Protection/Sanitation: (see Table 2): Skin: Prevent skin contact Eyes: Prevent eye contact Wash skin: When contam Remove: When wet (flamm) Change: N.R. Provide: Eyewash Quick drench. Physical Description: Colorless or pale-blue liquid or gas (above 78°F) with a bitter, almond-like odor. [Note: Often used as a 96% solution in water.].
